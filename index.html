About Us

Welcome to the heart of mountain biking excitement - our club is a tight-knit community passionate about the exhilarating world of enduro downhill and dirt jumping. Nestled in the midst of breathtaking landscapes, we thrive on the adrenaline rush that comes from tackling challenging terrains and soaring through the air on dirt jumps.


Our club is a hub for riders of all skill levels, from beginners eager to learn, to seasoned pros seeking the next big thrill. We offer a supportive environment where camaraderie and skill development go hand in hand. Whether you're looking to push your limits on treacherous downhill tracks, perfect your technique on dirt jumps, or simply share your love for the sport, you'll find your place here.


Join us for heart-pounding adventures, skill-building sessions, and unforgettable rides that will etch memories for a lifetime. Our experienced riders are always ready to offer guidance and encouragement, fostering an atmosphere of growth and achievement.


If you're ready to dive into the world of mountain biking that revolves around enduro downhill and dirt jumping, our club is the perfect place for you. Let's embark on this thrilling journey together, conquering obstacles, and experiencing the pure joy of the ride.
